How to get Miniswap token MINI

We kick-start MiniSwap by initiating MiniSwap ecology establishment funds and rewarding to our community. MiniSwap has a limited supply of 1,000,000,000 MINI, which will be used as start-up capital. These MINI will be given to the community according to the establishment rewards. In particular, 157,000,000 of the total supply will be issued in the form of private placement.

The fund pool uses a ladder exchange algorithm:

In the first round of private placement, 1ETH can be exchanged for 100901MINI, with a total of 2018039 pieces.

In the second round of private placement, 1ETH can be exchanged for 93838MINI, with a total of 2815164 pieces.

In the tenth round of private placement, 1ETH can be exchanged for 52510MINI, with a total of 5776154 pieces.

And so on...
